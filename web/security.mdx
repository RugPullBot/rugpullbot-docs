# Security

Protect your RugPullBot account with these security features.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security to your account.

### Enabling 2FA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Go to Profile > Security Settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    Use an authenticator app to scan the QR code
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Code">
    Enter the 6-digit code from your app
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Backup Codes">
    Store backup codes securely for recovery
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Supported Apps

- Google Authenticator
- Authy
- Microsoft Authenticator
- 1Password
- Any TOTP-compatible app

### 2FA Warning Banner

If 2FA is not enabled, you'll see a warning banner reminding you to secure your account.

<Warning>
Enable 2FA to protect your account from unauthorized access.
</Warning>

## Password Security

### Strong Password Tips

- Use at least 12 characters
- Mix uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers and symbols
- Avoid common words or patterns
- Don't reuse passwords

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to Profile > Change Password
2. Enter current password
3. Create new strong password
4. Confirm new password

## Session Security

### Active Sessions

Monitor your active login sessions:

- Device type
- Location (approximate)
- Last activity time

### Ending Sessions

Click "Sign Out" to end your current session. All other sessions remain active unless individually terminated.

## Security Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Unique Passwords">
    Never reuse your RugPullBot password on other sites.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Enable 2FA">
    Two-factor authentication significantly reduces account compromise risk.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Verify URLs">
    Always check you're on rugpullbot.com before logging in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Secure Your Email">
    Your email is used for recovery - keep it secure.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monitor Activity">
    Check your activity log regularly for suspicious actions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Anti-MEV & Anti-Sniper

In-simulation security features:

### Anti-MEV Protection
Protects transactions from sandwich attacks by malicious bots.

### Anti-Sniper Mode  
Prevents known sniper bots from front-running your launch.

<Note>
These features are enabled by default and highly recommended to keep on.
</Note>

## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability:

1. **Do not** publicly disclose the issue
2. Contact support via Telegram
3. Provide detailed information
4. Allow time for investigation

<Tip>
Responsible disclosure of security issues is appreciated and may be rewarded.
</Tip>

## Account Recovery

### Lost Password

1. Click "Forgot Password" on sign-in page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

### Lost 2FA Access

1. Use one of your backup codes
2. If no backup codes, contact support
3. Verify identity through support process

<Warning>
Store your 2FA backup codes securely. Without them, account recovery requires manual verification.
</Warning>
