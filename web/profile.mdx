# Profile & Account

The Profile page is your central hub for managing account settings, connected accounts, email, password, and two-factor authentication.

## Accessing Profile

Click on your username in the navigation bar and select "Profile" from the dropdown.

## Account Information

Your profile displays:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Email | Your email address with verification status |
| Full Name | Your display name |
| Member Since | Account creation date |
| Account ID | Your unique account identifier |
| Referral Code | Your personal referral code for sharing |

## Subscription Status

View your current subscription at the top of the profile:

- Plan name and tier badge
- Active/Inactive status
- Activation and expiration dates
- Days remaining
- Purchased add-ons

Click the plan badge to navigate to the [Plans](/web/plans) page for upgrades.

## Connected Accounts

Link social accounts to your profile for faster sign-in and account recovery. Supported providers:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Google" icon="google">
    Link your Google account for one-click sign-in.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Discord" icon="discord">
    Connect your Discord account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Steam" icon="steam">
    Link your Steam account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Telegram" icon="paper-plane">
    Connect via the Telegram bot. Once linked, Telegram cannot be disconnected.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Linking an Account

Click **Connect** next to any unlinked provider. You'll be redirected to that provider's login page, then back to your profile once linked.

### Unlinking an Account

Click **Disconnect** to remove a linked provider.

<Warning>
You cannot disconnect a provider if it's your only sign-in method. Make sure you have a password set or another provider linked first.
</Warning>

<Note>
Telegram connections are permanent and cannot be disconnected from the profile page.
</Note>

## Email Management

### Adding or Changing Email

If you signed up via SSO without an email, or need to update your email:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter New Email">
    Type your new email address in the email field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Verification Code">
    Click "Send Code" to receive a 6-digit verification code at that address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Code">
    Enter the code from your inbox to verify ownership.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your email is used for:
- Account recovery and password resets
- Important notifications
- Required before setting a password

## Password Management

### Setting a Password (SSO Users)

If you signed up via SSO, you may not have a password yet. To set one:

1. Make sure you have a **verified email address** first
2. Click **Set Password** in the password section
3. Enter and confirm your new password

### Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Password Section">
    Find the password section in your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Current Password">
    Type your current password for verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Create a new password meeting the requirements below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Re-enter the new password to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

## Redeem Code

Enter subscription or add-on codes:

1. Enter code in the "Redeem Code" field
2. Click "Redeem"
3. Plan or add-on activates immediately

<Tip>
Redeem codes are case-insensitive but must be entered exactly as provided.
</Tip>
