# Profile

The Profile page allows you to manage your account settings and preferences.

## Accessing Profile

Click on your username in the navigation bar and select "Profile" from the dropdown.

## Account Information

### Username
Your unique identifier on the platform. Displayed in:
- Navigation bar
- Rug activity feed (when publishing)
- Leaderboards

### Email
Email address associated with your account. Used for:
- Account recovery
- Notifications
- Verification

<Note>
Contact support if you need to change your email address.
</Note>

## Telegram Connection

### Connecting Telegram

If not connected, you'll see the option to link your Telegram account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click "Connect to Telegram" in your profile
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Telegram Bot">
    Click the link to open our Telegram bot
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Bot">
    Send /start to the bot
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Code">
    Enter the verification code shown
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Benefits of Connection

- Access to Portfolio features
- Real-time notifications
- Telegram bot commands
- Push alerts

### Disconnecting

To disconnect Telegram:
1. Go to Profile settings
2. Click "Disconnect Telegram"
3. Confirm disconnection

<Warning>
Disconnecting Telegram will disable Portfolio access and bot features.
</Warning>

## Password Management

### Change Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Change Password">
    Navigate to Profile > Change Password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Current">
    Type your current password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New">
    Create a new password meeting requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Re-enter the new password
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

## Subscription Information

View your current subscription status:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Plan | Current subscription tier |
| Status | Active/Expired |
| Features | List of included features |

Click on the plan badge to navigate to the Plans page for upgrades.

## Redeem Code

Enter subscription or addon codes:

1. Enter code in the "Redeem Code" field
2. Click "Redeem"
3. Plan/addon activates immediately

<Tip>
Redeem codes are case-insensitive but must be entered exactly.
</Tip>
