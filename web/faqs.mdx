# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RugPullBot.

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is RugPullBot?">
    RugPullBot is a platform for launching and managing cryptocurrency tokens on the Solana blockchain. It uses data from real historical market events to help you optimize your trading strategy.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I create an account?">
    Visit [rugpullbot.com/signup](https://rugpullbot.com/signup) and either fill in your details (username, email, password) or click one of the SSO buttons to sign up instantly with Google, Discord, Steam, or Telegram. Email signups require a 6-digit verification code sent to your inbox.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I sign in with Google, Discord, or Steam?">
    Yes. RugPullBot supports SSO sign-in with Google, Discord, Steam, and Telegram. If you create an account via SSO, you'll be asked to choose a username on your first login. You can link additional providers later from your [Profile](/web/profile) page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Telegram connection required?">
    Telegram connection is required for Portfolio features (creating and managing coins). However, you can browse the platform, view the Rugs feed, and explore plans without connecting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the subscription costs?">
    Plans range from 2 SOL (Tester) to 9 SOL (Premium). Most plans are one-time lifetime payments with no recurring fees. Boosted variants and add-ons are also available. See the [Plans](/web/plans) page for full details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Coin Creation & Trading

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What does 'Let AI Handle It' do?">
    This feature automatically fills in your coin's metadata (name, ticker, description, image, social links) from the latest detected rug in our queue. Coins created this way are marked with an "AI Mode" badge and use data from real historical market patterns.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is AI Mode?">
    AI Mode is a fully automated system that handles the entire coin lifecycle — from creation to rug execution to profit withdrawal — without manual intervention. It requires a minimum of 40 SOL in wallet balance and is available on Basic plans and above. See [AI Mode](/web/ai-mode) for full details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the trading work?">
    Trading uses data from actual rug pull events. Price action, volume, and market cap progression are based on real historical token behavior.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a rug trigger?">
    Rug triggers automatically execute the rug (sell all holdings) when specific conditions are met, such as reaching a certain market cap, holder count, or liquidity level.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I run multiple coins?">
    Yes, you can create and run multiple coins simultaneously. Each coin has its own settings and runs independently.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Wallet & Funds

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are the wallets real Solana wallets?">
    Yes, wallets created in RugPullBot are real Solana wallets. You can send and receive actual SOL and execute real trades on the blockchain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where do profits go?">
    Profits are sent to your configured withdrawal addresses. Set these in Coin Settings before launching. You can split profits across multiple wallets by percentage or fixed amount.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What fees are involved?">
    - **Solana network fees**: ~0.000005 SOL per transaction
    - **XMR conversion fee**: 5% on all profit when using XMR withdrawals
    - **Platform fee**: 10% mandatory fee on all rugs that profit more than $1,000 USD
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the platform fee work?">
    For any rug that generates more than $1,000 USD in profit, a mandatory 10% fee applies. You will receive notifications via the website, email, and Telegram bot with the exact SOL amount and wallet address for payment. This fee must be paid within 24 hours of the rug completing. Failure to pay results in account access being revoked and permanent blacklisting from the platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the bonding curve?">
    The bonding curve represents liquidity progress. On Pump.fun, approximately 85 SOL fills the curve to 100%. Your dev buy and bundler configuration affect initial curve position.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between bundler modes?">
    - **Safe**: Slower, maximum protection against detection
    - **Instant**: All wallets execute simultaneously for maximum impact
    - **Delayed**: Staggered execution for natural trading appearance
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is InstaRUG?">
    InstaRUG is a preset that analyzes the original rug's data and automatically configures your coin to rug at the same market cap as the original, using optimized settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Anti-MEV protection work?">
    Anti-MEV protection routes transactions through secure channels to avoid sandwich attacks from malicious bots that could front-run your trades.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I enable 2FA?">
    Go to your Profile page and find the Two-Factor Authentication section. Click "Enable", scan the QR code with an authenticator app, enter the verification code, and save your recovery codes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I lose access to my 2FA app?">
    Use one of your recovery codes (format: XXXX-XXXX) on the sign-in screen. If you don't have recovery codes, contact support via Telegram for manual verification.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I link my Google/Discord/Steam account?">
    Go to your [Profile](/web/profile) page and scroll to the Connected Accounts section. Click "Connect" next to the provider you want to link. You'll be redirected to that provider's login page, then back to your profile once linked.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I signed up with SSO — how do I set a password?">
    Go to your [Profile](/web/profile) page. First make sure you have a verified email address, then click "Set Password" in the password section.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my username or email?">
    You can update your email from the Profile page using the email verification flow. Username changes are not currently supported — contact support for special circumstances.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    Contact support to request account deletion. Note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
