# Sign Up

Getting started with RugPullBot is quick and easy. Follow these steps to create your account.

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign Up Page">
    Navigate to [rugpullbot.com/signup](https://rugpullbot.com/signup) to begin the registration process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details">
    Fill in your username, email address, and create a secure password. Your password must meet our security requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Telegram (Optional)">
    For full access to portfolio features, connect your account to Telegram. This enables you to receive real-time notifications and access the Telegram bot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Requirements

- **Username**: 3-20 characters, alphanumeric with underscores allowed
- **Email**: Valid email address for verification and recovery
- **Password**: Minimum 8 characters with at least one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character

## Telegram Connection

Connecting your Telegram account unlocks additional features:

- Access to the Portfolio section
- Real-time rug notifications
- Telegram bot commands
- Push notifications for important events

<Warning>
Some features like the Portfolio are only available after connecting your Telegram account.
</Warning>
