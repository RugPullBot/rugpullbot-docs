# Sign Up

Getting started with RugPullBot is quick and easy. Create your account using email or sign in instantly with a social provider.

## Sign Up with Email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign Up Page">
    Navigate to [rugpullbot.com/signup](https://rugpullbot.com/signup) to begin the registration process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details">
    Fill in your first name, last name, username, email address, and create a secure password. Optionally enter a referral code if you have one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for a 6-digit verification code and enter it on the verification screen to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Telegram (Optional)">
    For full access to portfolio features, connect your account to Telegram. This enables you to receive real-time notifications and access the Telegram bot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign Up with SSO

You can also create an account instantly using a social provider:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Google" icon="google">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Discord" icon="discord">
    Sign in with your Discord account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Steam" icon="steam">
    Sign in with your Steam account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Telegram" icon="paper-plane">
    Sign in via the Telegram login widget.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

When signing up via SSO for the first time, you'll be asked to **choose a username** before your account is created.

<Note>
If you sign up via SSO, you won't have a password initially. You can set one later from your [Profile](/web/profile) page after verifying an email address.
</Note>

## Account Requirements

- **Username**: 3-20 characters, alphanumeric with underscores and hyphens allowed
- **Email**: Valid email address for verification and recovery
- **Password**: Minimum 8 characters with at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character

## Telegram Connection

Connecting your Telegram account unlocks additional features:

- Access to the Portfolio section
- Real-time rug notifications
- Telegram bot commands
- Push notifications for important events

<Warning>
Some features like the Portfolio are only available after connecting your Telegram account.
</Warning>
