## Purchasing a Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Plan">
    Navigate to Plans and choose your tier
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Details">
    Confirm features and price
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Payment">
    Send SOL to the provided address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Plan activates automatically after payment confirmation
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lifetime Access

All plans except Tester include **lifetime access**:

- One-time payment
- No recurring fees
- All future updates included
- Permanent feature access

<Note>
Tester plan is designed for trial use and may have limited duration.
</Note>

## Upgrading

You can upgrade your plan at any time:

1. Go to Plans page
2. Select higher tier
3. Pay the difference
4. Features activate immediately
